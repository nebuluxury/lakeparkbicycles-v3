What we do

Two things, done right.

The $99 tune-up

The full works - safety check, brakes, gears, wheel true, drivetrain clean and lube. We pick your bike up free, tune it, and bring it back within 72 hours. Pedal bicycles only, any make and any age - e-bike service is handled separately under repairs.

Rentals, delivered

Men's and women's bikes plus e-bikes, delivered to your hotel, condo or home with helmet and lock included. Free delivery on rentals of 3 days or more; otherwise a flat $19, or pick up at the shop free.

Repairs, any make

Fifty years of wrench time on every brand - including e-bike motors and batteries. Flats, brakes, gears, full overhauls. Clear prices, free basic adjustments, always.
