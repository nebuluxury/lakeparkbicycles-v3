Our story

Fifty years of two wheels.

Lake Park Bicycles opened its doors in 1975 and never left. Through every trend - BMX, mountain, carbon, and now electric - we've stayed exactly what a neighborhood bike shop should be: a place where a real mechanic knows your name, sets your bike up right, and stands behind it.

Right now the shop itself is getting a full reimagining - new look, new layout, new everything. While that happens, we've taken the show on the road: tune-ups picked up and delivered free, and rental bikes brought straight to your door. From Jupiter to Lake Park, whatever you ride, we'll keep you moving.
